Instructions

  1. Rinse and drain the beans well. In a large bowl, combine the beans, red onion, cucumber, and parsley.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
