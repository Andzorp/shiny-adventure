    Instructions

    1. Rinse the toor dal thoroughly and pressure cook until soft.
    2. Heat ghee or oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
    3. Add chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté until the onions turn golden brown.
    4. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, and garam masala. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the oil starts to separate.
    5. Add the cooked dal and stir well. Adjust salt to taste.
    6. Let it simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
    7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or chapati.
