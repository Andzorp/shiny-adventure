    Instructions

    1. Soak chickpeas in water overnight. Drain well.
    2. In a food processor, combine chickpeas, onion, parsley, cilantro, garlic, cumin, coriander, baking powder, and salt. Process until mixture is coarse but holds together.
    3. Shape mixture into small balls or patties.
    4. Heat oil in a pan and fry falafel until golden brown and crispy.
    5. Serve falafel with pita bread, tahini sauce, or hummus.
