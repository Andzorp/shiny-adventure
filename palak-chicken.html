    Instructions

    1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
    2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft. Stir in cumin, coriander, turmeric, and salt.
    3. Add chicken and cook until browned. Add chopped spinach and cook until the chicken is done.
    4. Serve hot with rice or roti.
