    Instructions

    1. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
    2. In a bowl, whisk together peanut butter, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, maple syrup, garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes.
    3. Toss the noodles in the peanut butter sauce until well coated.
    4. Garnish with chopped peanuts and serve immediately.
