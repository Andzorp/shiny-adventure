    Instructions

    1. In a food processor, combine chickpeas, lemon juice, and tahini. Process until smooth.
    2. Scrape down the sides of the bowl and add garlic, olive oil, cumin, and salt. Process until well blended and smooth.
    3. If the hummus is too thick, add water a tablespoon at a time until the desired consistency is reached.
    4. Transfer to a serving bowl and drizzle with additional olive oil. Garnish with chopped parsley.
    5. Serve with pita bread or vegetables.
